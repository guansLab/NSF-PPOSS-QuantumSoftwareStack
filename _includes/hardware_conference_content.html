Workshop on Quantum Applications

Details

This is the second of three invited workshops to inform the potential path towards the challenges in developing a full software stack for a cluster of heterogeneous Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum (NISQ) machines. This workshop focuses on quantum computing hardware and their requirements for the full-stack. The outcome will be a comprehensive report to the community that will span quantum applications, software stack and hardware.
