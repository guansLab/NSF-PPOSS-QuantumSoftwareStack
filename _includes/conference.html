Quantum Applications Conference

Details

This conference targets research efforts for applications of quantum computing in other fields (e.g. chemistry simulations, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, image processing, etc.). Talks and demonstrations will provide attendees with insights into the domains that are benefitting from quantum computing and the techniques/algorithms that are enabling novel innovations. Topics and discussions will be compiled into conference proceedings to describe the state of the art for applied quantum computing.

This is the first of three conferences organized by the PIs targetting applications of quantum computing, compilation techniques for quantum circuits, and advances in quantum hardware technologies.

Logistics

When: November 6, 2023
Where: Courtyard by Mariott next to Case Western Reserve University
